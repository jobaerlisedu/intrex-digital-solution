F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including finance, healthcare, education, retail, and small to mid-sized enterprises seeking digital transformation..

Can I customize the services based on my business needs?

Absolutely. Our solutions are fully customizable — whether it’s network setup, cloud deployment, or IT management, we tailor everything to your requirements.

How secure are your cloud and network services?

Security is our top priority. We implement end-to-end encryption, multi-layered firewalls, and 24/7 monitoring to ensure your data is protected.

Do you offer on-site and remote support?

Yes, we provide both on-site and remote IT support depending on your location and the nature of the issue or service request.

What is included in your IT Managed Services?

Our IT Managed Services include system monitoring, preventive maintenance, technical support, backup management, and performance optimization.

How do I get started with Intrex Digital Solution Hub?

Getting started is simple — contact us through our website, email, or phone. We'll schedule a consultation to assess your needs and propose the right solutions.
